Ornate Shop Module

Combines Collapsibe Product Categories and Product Multi-image

Ornate Shop Module is a specially desinged module which combines Collapsibe Product Categories and Product Multi-image in just one module along side with elegant homepage design.

Ornate Shop Module has also included the collapsible product categories in wesite shop section. It doesnot matter how many product categories you have you will get a nice collapsible look with all the parent an child categories

Cleaner Multi Product Images

Showcase all your product images with a cleaner multi product feature. Add multiple images to product and the module will provide you a cleaner view of all the images with a image slider in the bottom of the main image area.

With product multi-image you can add as many images to your product. All images will be shown in a slider under the main image where you can choose images for full view.

Simply elegant Shop in Website with Collapsible Product Categories

Modified shop section will give you a clean and fine look. It will gives you a unfussy look to your shop section. We have collapsible product categories with the shop which will take any number of product categories with parent and child category view.
